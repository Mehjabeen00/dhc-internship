Our Story

In 2015, our founder Carlos Mendez was running two dry van trucks out of Houston when he realized that the biggest obstacle to growing his fleet wasn't capital — it was time. Finding good-paying loads, negotiating rates, handling paperwork, and managing broker relationships took so much time that there was barely any left to actually run the business.

He sold one truck and used the proceeds to hire his first dispatcher. Within six months, his remaining truck was earning 40% more than when he had two trucks. The math was undeniable.

Today, HaulMaster employs 85+ dedicated dispatchers serving 500+ carriers across 47 states. We handle over 2,400 loads per month and have built lasting relationships with 1,200+ verified freight brokers.

Services

Full-Service
Dispatch Solutions

From finding the load to delivering the paperwork, we handle every step so you can focus on what you do best — driving.

📦

Load Booking

Our dispatchers work around the clock searching load boards (DAT, Truckstop, Amazon Relay, and more) plus our exclusive broker network to find the highest-paying loads for your lanes and equipment type.

  • Access to 50+ premium load boards
  • Dedicated broker relationships for exclusive loads
  • Rate negotiation — we fight for every dollar
  • Equipment-specific matching (FTL, LTL, Flatbed, Reefer, Hotshot)
  • Lane analysis and backhaul optimization
🗺️

Route Planning & Optimization

Smart routing that considers fuel stops, rest areas, weigh stations, traffic patterns, and HOS regulations. We plan your trips to maximize productivity while keeping you safe and compliant.

  • HOS-compliant route generation
  • Fuel optimization and stop planning
  • Real-time traffic and weather routing
  • Deadhead minimization strategies
  • Multi-stop load consolidation
🎧

24/7 Driver Support

Emergencies don't keep business hours. Our dispatchers are available around the clock to handle breakdowns, accidents, detention issues, lumper fees, and any situation that arises on the road.

  • 24/7/365 phone and text support
  • Breakdown and emergency coordination
  • Detention time tracking and collection
  • Lumper fee pre-authorization
  • Real-time broker communication
📄

Documentation Handling

Complete end-to-end paperwork management. From rate confirmations to proof of delivery, we ensure every document is properly handled, filed, and submitted on time.

  • Rate confirmation review and negotiation
  • BOL preparation and management
  • POD collection and submission
  • Invoice generation and factoring setup
  • FMCSA compliance document management

Dispatch Operations Center

Our operations center monitors your loads in real-time, communicates proactively with receivers, and handles any complications before they become problems — so you never get caught off guard.

  • Real-time load tracking and status updates

  • Load issue resolution
  • Weekly performance reporting
